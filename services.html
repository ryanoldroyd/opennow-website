Insurance & Payment

Transparent pricing — no surprise bills

We're a fee-for-service practice, which means payment is collected at the time of your visit. You'll always know your estimate before we start. If your plan includes out-of-network benefits, your insurance may reimburse you directly — we'll give you the documentation you need to submit a claim.

Out-of-Network Insurance

Many insurance plans include out-of-network benefits. We'll provide an itemized receipt so you can submit for reimbursement according to your plan's coverage.

No Insurance? No Problem.

Ask about our in-house membership plan with discounted rates on exams, cleanings, and restorative care.
