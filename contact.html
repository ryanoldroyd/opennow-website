FAQ

Quick answers to common questions

Can I really get an appointment the same day?

Most of the time, yes — especially for emergencies. We hold open slots every day we're open specifically for same-day calls. The earlier you call, the better the chance.

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major PPO plans and file claims for you. Call us with your member ID and we'll verify your benefits before your visit. If you don't have insurance, ask about our in-house membership plan.

What counts as a dental emergency?

Severe or persistent toothache, swelling in the face or gums, a cracked or knocked-out tooth, a lost filling or crown, bleeding that won't stop, or any trauma to the mouth. When in doubt — call us.

Are you open during weekday emergencies?

Our scheduled hours are Friday evenings and weekends. For weekday emergencies, leave a voicemail or send Ashley an email — we'll reach out and help you find appropriate care.

Do you see kids?

Yes! We see kids of all ages and most adults find weekend visits much easier than pulling kids out of school. Our team is trained to make first visits calm and positive.

How much will my visit cost?

It depends on your insurance and what's needed, but we always provide a clear estimate before any treatment begins. You'll never be surprised by a bill.

I'm anxious about the dentist. Can you help?

Absolutely. We see a lot of patients who've been putting it off. We offer nitrous oxide, oral sedation options, and — most importantly — a team that won't make you feel judged. Tell us about your concerns when you book.

Need help right now?

Tap to call — we'll answer if we're open, or call you back fast if it's after hours.
