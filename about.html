Our Story

The dental practice that opens when you actually need it.

We're a small, locally owned office in Riverton, Utah — focused on giving people in the south Salt Lake valley a better way to take care of their teeth.

Why we exist

Most people don't have a free Tuesday.

Open Now Dental started with a simple observation: the people who need dentists most — working parents, shift workers, students, families — almost never have weekday afternoons free. So they put it off. And small problems become big ones.

We flipped the schedule. Instead of asking patients to fit us in, we built our hours around the times most people are actually free: Friday after work, Saturday and Sunday. The result is a calmer practice, shorter waits, and a lot fewer patients walking in already in pain.

If you've been putting off the dentist because the timing never works — that's exactly the problem we're here to solve.
